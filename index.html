How this program works

Welcome, learner. Take two minutes to read this — it explains everything you need, including how to document your work.

The learning path

  • There are 4 modules. Work through them in order — finishing one unlocks the next.
  • Each module mixes short lessons, interactive drills, a hands-on simulation, and a final knowledge check.
  • Score 70% or higher on the check to complete a module. Retake it as often as you like — no penalty.
  • Everything is written for someone starting from zero. We build up slowly before anything technical.

Saving & resuming

  • Tap Save progress any time to download a small .json file — your personal bookmark.
  • To continue later, return to the welcome screen and use Load progress with that same file.
